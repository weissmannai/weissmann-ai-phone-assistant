## 1. SaaS AI Bots: The Self-Service Model

Most AI phone assistants on the market are offered as SaaS (Software-as-a-Service).

Typical characteristics:
- Self-setup via dashboard
- Generic AI models reused across industries
- Limited understanding of restaurant operations
- “Message-taking” instead of active reservation logic
- Responsibility lies entirely with the restaurant

### Common limitations of SaaS AI bots
- ❌ Missed or incomplete bookings during peak hours  
- ❌ Poor handling of accents, dialects, and noisy environments  
- ❌ No real sales logic (upselling, event promotion, table optimization)  
- ❌ No human optimization after launch  
- ❌ One-size-fits-all configuration  

For hospitality businesses, this often leads to **disappointment after the initial demo**.
